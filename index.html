Leanne Hyndman

Leanne helps clients to connect with their core values, thoughts and feelings, history and purpose. Her extensive education along with her authentic, creative approach enables clients to feel supported and challenged to create lasting and meaningful change.

Leanne’s work has been described as compassionate, judgement-free, insightful, empathetic, humorous, effective and deeply connecting.

Leanne has worked with adults, children and groups within schools, private practice, community organisations and employment assistance programs. She has specialized experience in working with road trauma, grief and bereavement.

Qualifications

  • Masters of Narrative Therapy & Community Work
  • Bachelor of Arts in Counselling
  • Post Graduate Certificate in Sandplay Therapy and Symbol Work
  • Clinical registration - PACFA (registration 26236)

Services

  • Individual counselling
  • Child and adolescent counselling
  • Parenting support
  • Consultant – Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
  • Facilitator of mindfulness skills
  • Sandplay – expressive arts – symbol work
  • Grief and Loss
  • Road trauma recovery
